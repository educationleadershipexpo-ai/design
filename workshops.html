Expo Agenda Overview

Two days. One mission — shaping the future of learning in Qatar.

Day 1 — The Future of Education Begins

Morning Highlights

  • Welcome breakfast & opening ceremony with the Ministry of Education
  • Keynote: “The Future of Education in the Digital Era”
  • Panel: AI & Innovation in Qatar’s Education Landscape

Midday Tracks

  • Workshops on modernizing LMS/ERP and protecting school data
  • Live EdTech pitch showcase featuring regional startups
  • VIP networking lunch with education leaders

Afternoon Sessions

  • Masterclasses on AI tools for educators & smart school management
  • Research spotlight: Blended learning trends across the GCC
  • Parent & student voice sessions: “What Learning Feels Like in 2025”

Day 2 — Empowering Leaders & Educators

Morning

  • Keynote: “Shaping the Schools of Tomorrow”
  • Panel: Digital Equity & Access in Education
  • Innovation walkthrough on the expo floor

Midday Workshops

  • Designing a Future-Proof Curriculum
  • Public-Private Collaboration in Education
  • University Impact Showcases

Afternoon

  • Teacher Empowerment through Technology
  • Financial Planning for School Growth
  • Leadership Circle: Future of Private Education in the GCC
  • EdTech Innovation Panel + Student Job of the Future Showcase

Closing Ceremony

  • Student Diwan Education Excellence Awards
  • Group Photo & Closing Remarks
